ABOUT US

The Math & Finance Club strives to recruit students across all schools and programs with the purpose of fostering an interest in Finance and providing a medium for members to navigate their way into the industry. Common activities include on-campus/off-campus competitions, industry professional info-sessions, alumni panels, and technical workshops.

QUARTERLY TRADING COMPETITIONS

Our club holds a trading competition during each quarter of the academic year. Here, you have the opportunity to experience real world trading with the chance to win prizes

CONNECTING YOU TO PROFESSIONALS

Networking is critical in the financial industry. One of our main goals with this club is to build a respectable reputation so that we can attain sponsorships from well-known companies. This is a new club and we need motivated individuals to help us on this journey.
