THE 2022 MFC QUARTERLY TRADING COMPETITION

The Math Finance Club at UCI will host a trading competition every quarter of the academic year. This competition is hosted on the trading simulator on Investopedia. Students enrolled in the competition will start with a balance of $100,000 and be free to trade any stocks and options on the market. The top three players with the highest portfolio will all win a cash prize.

General Rules

  • Every student involved must be enrolled in an accredited college or university at the undergraduate or graduate level
  • All participants must be enrolled for the duration of the competition at the university
  • A student who was part of a winning team in the previous quarter competition is not eligible to win cash prices during the current year's competition
  • Participants agree to provide true information during registration. If a participant becomes eligible for any prize, the informatino provided during his/her registration will be used as confirmation. If any information provided by a participant is deemed incorrect, they will not recieve any prize even if they are to win one
  • Every participant should have only one unqiue nickname and email linked with his/her personal details, which will be used for that specific account. Opening proxy servers may result in the relevant participant's disqualification
  • Participants shall acknowledge and accept that, in case they are awarded with a prize by MFC, they may be asked to participate in promotional events, interviews, public announcements in connection with MFC. Official results of every month will be published
